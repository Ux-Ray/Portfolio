Improving How Users Apply and Succeed on LinkedIn

Many users struggle to manage their job applications on LinkedIn and often feel uncertain about the platform’s ability to support them effectively. Our goal was to build features that simplify the job search experience, increase transparency, and boost users’ confidence.
